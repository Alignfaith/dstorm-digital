What We Deliver

Every service, spelled out.

🌐 Marketing Website

Custom-coded marketing sites that load fast, look sharp, and convert visitors. No page builders, no WordPress bloat — just clean HTML, CSS, and JavaScript delivered to Vercel or GitHub Pages.

⚡ Web Application

Full-stack web apps built with Next.js, React, TypeScript, and PostgreSQL. Auth, payments, APIs, dashboards — we build the complete product and hand you the keys.

🍎 iOS App

React Native iOS apps built for the App Store. We handle the build, the Apple Developer enrollment, and the submission — you get a live app in your users' hands.

🤖 Android App

Android apps deployed to the Google Play Store. Cross-platform or native — we'll recommend the right approach based on your budget and timeline.

🔗 GitHub Hosting Setup

We set up your repository, configure deployments to Vercel or Netlify, and document how to make future updates yourself. You own everything from day one.

🛡️ Backup & Maintenance

Monthly maintenance plan that keeps your site secure, fast, and up to date. Includes security patches, dependency updates, uptime monitoring, and a small bank of hours for minor changes.
